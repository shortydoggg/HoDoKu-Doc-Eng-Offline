HoDoKu Solving Technique Index: Example for "Dual 2-String Kite"

Original sudoku:

32..479.6..6........5.9..3.....7.......9.1..5..28..7.........7.6.3..4....5..26143

Use the following line if you want to load the sudoku in HoDoKu:

:0401:1:32.+5479.6..6+2+1+3.+5..+45+69+8+23.+5..+47+2.....+79.1.+25..28.+57..+2+1+4+3+5+9+67+86+73+1+84+5+9+2.5.+726143::149 168:

| 48   368   7   | 9  36  1 | 348  2    5   |

| 149  369   2   | 8  36  5 | 7    16   149 |

| 6    7     3   | 1  8   4 | 5    9    2   |

| 89   5     89  | 7  2   6 | 1    4    3   |
